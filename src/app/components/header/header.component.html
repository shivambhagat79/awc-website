A Walk on the Wild Side of IIT Ropar

A closer look at the wild animals that call our campus home.

Join us on a journey through the lush greenery of IIT Ropar, where nature flourishes and a diverse range of wild animals call it home. Explore the beauty and serenity of the campus as we showcase stunning photographs capturing the essence of our unique wildlife community. From graceful birds to playful squirrels, each shot is a testament to the natural wonders that inhabit our grounds. So come, take a walk on the wild side with us, and be mesmerized by the beauty of IIT Ropar's wildlife."
